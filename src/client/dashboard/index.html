This overlay combines Lower Dual + Sponsors + Ticker in a single output. Control each element from its individual tab.

Information

Layers (z-index):
Sponsors z-index: 10 (top left)
Lower Dual z-index: 20 (bottom both sides)
Ticker z-index: 30 (bottom, 65px height)

The "Show All" toggle emits SHOW for all three components simultaneously. Use it to turn the entire combo on or off from OBS.
